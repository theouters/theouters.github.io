background info

The Outers are a London based indie rock band. Their songs are characterised by a distinctive, up-tempo rhythm section supporting catchy guitar and vocal melodies. "We try to incorporate the vintage sound and subtle melodies of British pop, and the depth of Post Punk with the edge of electro pop to add a bit of a dance groove.
The Outers manage to find their style after mixing their influences such as the Arctic Monkeys, Metric, Grimes, Temples, Editors, Chvrches, The Strokes, The Killers, Suede, Coldplay, Blondie and The Cure.
