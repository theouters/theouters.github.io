background info

The Outers are a London based indie rock/post punk band with original music written by Phill Dodane and Ade Seriki, formed in 2013. Their songs are characterised by a distinctive, up-tempo rhythm section supporting strong guitar and vocal melodies."We try to incorporate the vintage sound and subtle melodies of British pop, with the edge of modern Indie Rock and the depth of Post Punk".
The Outers manage to find their style after mixing their influences such as Suede, The Smiths, Stone Roses, Metric, MGMT, Kings of Leon, Blondie, The Killers, The Strokes, The Editiors and Coldplay. "Our love for Indie-Rock bands, London's eclectic culture and life style inspires us".

The Outers are:

  • Ade Seriki (singer-song writer / synth)
  • Phil Dodane (song writer, guitars)
  • Filippo Mortari (bass)
  • Ersen Sener (drums)
